# Heatwave Disaster Scam Taxonomy

This document outlines the 6 primary scam categories tailored specifically for the Indian heatwave context, based on real-world patterns identified in I4C reports and CERT-In advisories.

## 1. Fake Heat-Relief Scheme (fake_relief_scheme)
* **Description:** Fraudsters fabricate central or state schemes (e.g., "PM Garmi Rahat Yojana") claiming to provide ₹5,000–₹10,000 in relief funds.
* **Precedent:** Similar to PM Kisan fund scams or COVID-19 relief phishing.
* **Pressure Tactic:** Urgency, Authority Impersonation.
* **Red Flags:** Unofficial URL (e.g., `pm-yojana-update.in`), asking for UPI PIN or Aadhaar OTP, unrealistic monetary amounts.
* **Correct Action:** Verify on official portals (`mygov.in`, `nic.in`); Report to 1930.

## 2. Fake Hospital/Ambulance (fake_hospital)
* **Description:** Scammers intercept or fake search results for 108 ambulance services, demanding advance UPI payments for heatstroke admission or dispatch.
* **Precedent:** Hospital admission fraud during COVID-19 oxygen crisis.
* **Pressure Tactic:** Extreme Urgency, Fear.
* **Red Flags:** Demanding advance payment before dispatching ambulance, personal UPI QR code sent via WhatsApp, unofficial 10-digit mobile number claiming to be 108 dispatch.
* **Correct Action:** Only dial the official 108 or 112 directly. Do not pay in advance for emergency dispatch.

## 3. Fake Electricity Bill / Cooler Subsidy (fake_subsidy)
* **Description:** SMS/WhatsApp claiming power will be cut off tonight due to unpaid bills unless a link is clicked, or offering fake subsidies on ACs/coolers.
* **Precedent:** Ongoing electricity bill disconnection scams rampant across India.
* **Pressure Tactic:** Scarcity, Fear.
* **Red Flags:** "Dear Customer your electricity power will be disconnected at 9.30 pm...", unknown numbers, unofficial app download requests (APK).
* **Correct Action:** Ignore SMS; check the official DISCOM app; Report to 1930.

## 4. Fake Water Tanker Scam (fake_water_tanker)
* **Description:** During severe water shortages, scammers pose as official municipal water board dispatchers and ask for a deposit to send a water tanker.
* **Precedent:** Urban water crisis exploitation (e.g., Delhi, Bangalore summer shortages).
* **Pressure Tactic:** Scarcity, Urgency.
* **Red Flags:** Payment demanded upfront for government tanker, strange UPI IDs (e.g., `waterboard@ybl`), unverified contact numbers.
* **Correct Action:** Book tankers only through official municipal portals/apps.

## 5. Fake NGO Heat Shelter Donation (fake_ngo)
* **Description:** Fraudsters create fake charity pages or WhatsApp forwards asking for donations to buy ORS, water coolers, or set up heat shelters for the poor.
* **Precedent:** Fake charity schemes exploiting disaster empathy.
* **Pressure Tactic:** Reciprocity, Urgency.
* **Red Flags:** No FCRA or 80G registration, generic UPI IDs (e.g., `helpindia@okaxis`), high-pressure emotional language.
* **Correct Action:** Donate only to verified NGOs or the PM CARES/CM Relief funds.

## 6. Fake Employer Heat-Allowance (fake_employer_allowance)
* **Description:** SMS targeted at daily-wage workers or NREGA laborers claiming the government or employer has deposited a "heat compensation" allowance, asking them to click a link to claim.
* **Precedent:** Wage theft and NREGA spoofing.
* **Pressure Tactic:** Authority Impersonation, Urgency.
* **Red Flags:** "Click here to receive ₹1000 daily wage bonus", unfamiliar links.
* **Correct Action:** Contact the employer or Gram Panchayat directly; do not click the link.
